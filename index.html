SMMT overview

SMMT means `SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER TOOLS`, a cleaner and more pro-looking workspace for planning, editing, saving, and managing social media operations in one app.

Full app name

The public-facing product name is `SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER TOOLS` while the short in-app brand is `SMMT`.

Features
  • per-user dashboard
  • post / library workflow
  • idea bank
  • asset uploads
  • AI Assistant
  • clients & team workspace
  • VIP membership locking
VIP locked tools

If VIP is not active, these premium tools should stay locked: `AI Assistant`, `Calendar`, and `Clients & Team`.

VIP membership pricing

VIP pricing is now set to `$3 / month`, `$27 / year`, and `$70 lifetime`.

Payment direction

The upgrade flow is now positioned for direct membership payment, with PayMongo as the next payment gateway stage.

Client & team upgrade
  • meeting rooms
  • chat account by account
  • voice and video calls
  • upload photos and videos
  • record, send, undo, emoji
Language support

The app now includes broader language selection so the interface direction can follow the user’s country or preferred language more closely.

Terms of service

Use the app responsibly, keep your account secure, and avoid uploading illegal or harmful content. Direct membership billing terms can be finalized once the payment gateway is switched to PayMongo.

Privacy policy

Basic account and workspace data are used only to operate the app experience. Collaboration privacy details for chat, calls, and uploads can be added in the next backend phase.

Missing gaps covered

This update also adds stronger naming consistency, premium locking rules, cleaner styling direction, and clearer separation between free tools and VIP tools.

Final direction

The app is now closer to a pro product look, with the remaining big backend step centered on live collaboration and PayMongo checkout.
