Common questions

Is this for beginners?

Yes — the workspace is organized around how a VA actually works day to day, so you can start with just Posts, Ideas, and Notes and add tools as you need them.

Can I use it on mobile and desktop?

Both. On a phone it runs full-width like a normal app; on a laptop/desktop browser it opens inside a phone-shaped frame so the same layout stays familiar everywhere.

Do you store my social media accounts?

No. VA Yarn! never asks for or stores social media logins. Platform fields are just labels/links you type in yourself.

How secure is my data?

Your data lives in your own Supabase project. Only you (and teammates you explicitly invite, at the role you choose) can access it.
